Frequently Asked Questions

Thank you for your interest in my research!

  • Prospective Ph.D. students: Please apply to our Ph.D. program by mid December and mention me as a potential advisor. This will ensure that I review your application. Emailing me will not affect your prospects.
  • Undergraduate and Masters students at Penn: Please apply via this form. You are welcome to apply at any time of the year. However, I only review and accept applicants at the start of Fall and Spring semesters.
  • Internships for students not at Penn: I do not offer internships to students outside Penn, including high-school students, students at other universities, etc.
  • Postdocs: I consider exceptional postdoc candidates based on availability of funding and research fit. I am currently looking for postdocs in the areas of AI for code, AI in medicine, and neuro-symbolic learning.
